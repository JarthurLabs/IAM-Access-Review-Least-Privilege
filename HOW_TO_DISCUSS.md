# How to Discuss This Repository

## Strong Explanation

This work shows an IAM governance workflow for access requests, role-based access, privileged access approval, MFA enforcement, and quarterly access reviews.

## Best Talking Points

- Access decisions are tied to job role and business need.
- Former user and excessive access issues are clearly identified.
- Privileged access is tracked separately.
- MFA requirements are mapped by system.
- Evidence is structured for audit and customer assurance.

## What Not to Claim

Do not claim this is a real SOX audit or production IAM assessment.
