# Quarterly Access Review Process

## Purpose

Confirm that user access remains appropriate based on job role, business need, and least privilege.

## Process

1. Export current access by system.
2. Compare access to the RBAC matrix.
3. Identify excessive, outdated, shared, or orphaned access.
4. Assign decisions: keep, reduce, remove, approve, or investigate.
5. Complete remediation.
6. Retain evidence.

## Review Decisions

| Decision | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Keep | Access matches job role |
| Reduce | Access is needed but permission level is too high |
| Remove | Access is no longer justified |
| Approve | Access is privileged or sensitive and requires explicit approval |
| Investigate | More information is needed |
