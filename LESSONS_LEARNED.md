# Lessons Learned

- IAM reviews are not just permission tables. Employment status and business need matter.
- Former users are often higher priority than slightly over-permissioned current users.
- Privileged access should be documented, not automatically treated as suspicious.
- RBAC only works if the business agrees with the role definitions.
- This project connects strongly to SaaS implementation because onboarding, permissions, admin roles, and training are all part of IAM operations.
